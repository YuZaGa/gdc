Vision

Global Design Cell

Empanelled as Architects and Consultants with Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MORT&H), New Delhi; registered with the Council of Architecture (COA) and associated with The Indian Institute of Architects (IIA), Global Design Cell is now two years old Architectural Consultancy based at New Delhi, India.

Established in 2012, Global Design Cell originated with the idea of building a team of highly skilled professionals located at different geographical locations yet who stay firmly connected and jointly cater to the needs of the clients spread globally.

Global Design Cell has so far worked on a variety of Residential, Public, and Commercial projects and it is growing fast with its skilled and motivated professionals who have experience of designing almost all kinds of buildings.

As a team, we are a small firm practicing thoughtful, specific, contemporary design for discriminating and highly involved clients.

The team consists of Architects, Engineers and allied Consultants who specializes in and provides consultancy in Architectural, Environmental, Project Management and Allied Engineering Services, including Structural, Civil, Public Health and Hygiene, Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing, Landscaping & Interiors.
